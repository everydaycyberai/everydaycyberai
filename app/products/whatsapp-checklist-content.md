## PART 1: PRIVACY SETTINGS (Do This Right Now!)

### Account Settings
- [ ] Settings > Account > Privacy > Last Seen: "My Contacts" or "Nobody"
- [ ] Profile Photo: "My Contacts" only
- [ ] About: "My Contacts" only
- [ ] Status: "My Contacts" only
- [ ] Read Receipts: OFF (optional)
- [ ] Groups: "My Contacts Only" — prevents strangers from adding you

### Two-Step Verification (MUST DO!)
- [ ] Settings > Account > Two-step verification > ENABLE
- [ ] Set a 6-digit PIN (different from UPI PIN)
- [ ] Add recovery email address
- [ ] Never share this PIN with anyone

### Fingerprint/Face Lock
- [ ] Settings > Privacy > Fingerprint Lock > ENABLE
- [ ] Timeout: 1 minute

## PART 2: SECURITY CHECKLIST

### Daily Habits
- [ ] Never share OTP received on WhatsApp with anyone
- [ ] Never click unknown links sent via WhatsApp
- [ ] Never scan unknown QR codes
- [ ] Verify sender before opening attachments

### Red Flags — Ignore/Block Immediately
- [ ] "You have won a prize — click this link"
- [ ] "Your KYC is expiring — share your details"
- [ ] "I am from bank/police/CBI — cooperate or you will be arrested"
- [ ] "Work from home — earn ₹50,000/month — pay ₹500 registration"
- [ ] Messages asking you to forward to win anything

### If Your Account is Hacked
1. Check if you can still access WhatsApp
2. If yes: Settings > Account > Security > Check linked devices
3. Log out all unknown devices
4. Enable Two-step verification immediately
5. Inform your contacts not to respond to messages from your account
6. If locked out: Reinstall WhatsApp > Enter your number > Verify via SMS

## PART 3: COMMON WHATSAPP SCAMS IN INDIA

### Scam 1: Fake Customer Care
How: You search for bank/company number on Google — fake number appears — they ask for OTP
Prevention: Always use number from official bank card or website

### Scam 2: Family Emergency Scam  
How: "Hi, this is your cousin/friend — I lost my phone, need ₹5000 urgent"
Prevention: Always call the actual person to verify

### Scam 3: Investment Scam
How: "Join our WhatsApp group — guaranteed 5% daily returns on crypto/stocks"
Prevention: No investment gives guaranteed daily returns — always a scam

### Scam 4: Job Fraud
How: "Work from home — like YouTube videos — earn ₹2000/day — pay ₹500 first"
Prevention: Legitimate jobs never ask you to pay first
